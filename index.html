FAQ

Common questions

Does it actually save data?
Yes. Unlike most "data savers" that just speed up loading, DataSaver NG physically reduces the size of files downloaded to your browser — starting with images, which account for most of a page's data weight.
Is it free?
Completely free. No subscription, no account required, no hidden fees. Built for Nigerians who pay too much for data.
Does it slow down my browser?
No — tracker blocking actually makes pages load faster since those scripts never download. Image compression happens on our server before the image reaches you.
Which networks does it work on?
All of them — MTN, Airtel, Glo, 9mobile. It works at the browser level so your network doesn't matter.
Is my data safe?
We don't store or log your browsing history. The proxy only compresses content as it passes through — nothing is saved on our servers.
